## Testnet-First Strategy

### Why Testnet?

**Testnet is completely free:**
- No gas fees
- No deployment costs
- Unlimited transactions
- Free testnet tokens from faucets
- Same functionality as mainnet

**Benefits:**
1. Prove concept without spending money
2. Build community and get users
3. Test everything thoroughly
4. Raise funding based on testnet traction
5. Lower risk (no real money at stake)

## Funding Strategies ($0 to Apply)

### 1. Grants (Completely Free to Apply)

**Blockchain Foundation Grants:**
- **Ethereum Foundation**: $10K-100K grants
  - Apply: https://esp.ethereum.foundation/
- **Solana Foundation**: $5K-50K grants
  - Apply: https://solana.org/grants
- **TON Foundation**: $5K-50K grants
  - Apply: https://ton.org/grants
- **Polygon**: $5K-25K grants
  - Apply: https://polygon.technology/grants
- **Base**: Grants for Base ecosystem
  - Apply: https://base.org/ecosystem

**Web3 Grants:**
- **Gitcoin**: Community grants
  - Apply: https://gitcoin.co/grants
- **Optimism**: Retroactive funding
  - Apply: https://optimism.io/retropgf
- **Arbitrum**: Ecosystem grants
  - Apply: https://arbitrum.io/grants

**How to Apply:**
1. Research each grant's requirements
2. Write compelling proposal (free)
3. Show technical progress (even if early)
4. Apply to 10-20 grants (increase chances)
5. **Cost**: $0 (completely free to apply)

### 2. Community Pre-Sale (After Mainnet)

**When**: After you deploy to mainnet (need $500-1K first)

**If Legal in Your Jurisdiction:**
- Sell tokens to community members
- Small amounts ($100-1,000 per person)
- Use smart contracts for transparency
- Target: $10K-50K from 50-200 people

**Legal Considerations:**
- Check local regulations (free research)
- May need accredited investors
- Consider SAFT (Simple Agreement for Future Tokens)
- Consult lawyer (free consultation available)

### 3. Revenue-Sharing Partnerships

**Partners:**
- GPU providers (share revenue)
- AI companies (they need compute)
- Universities (research partnerships)

**Model**: 
- No upfront payment
- Share revenue 50/50 or 70/30
- Win-win for both parties
- **Cost**: $0

## Development Strategy ($0)

### Phase 1: MVP on Testnet (Days 1-90)

**What to Build:**
- Basic smart contract (ERC-20 token + simple marketplace)
- Basic backend (Python or Node.js)
- Simple web dashboard (React/Next.js)
- Basic GPU provider client (Python)
- Simple Python SDK

**What to Skip:**
- Multi-chain (start with one chain - Ethereum testnet)
- TEE integration (too complex)
- ZK proofs (too complex)
- Mobile apps (use responsive web)
- Advanced features (add later)

**Timeline**: 30-60 days with 2-3 people
**Cost**: $0 (everything is free on testnet)

### Phase 2: Beta on Testnet (Days 31-90)

**What to Add:**
- Basic quality assurance (redundant execution)
- Simple task matching
- Basic analytics
- Community features

**Cost**: $0 (still on testnet)

### Phase 3: Launch on Testnet (Day 90)

**What to Have:**
- Working product on testnet (not perfect)
- 50-100 testnet users
- Basic security (community review)
- Community support

**Cost**: $0 (testnet launch is free)
